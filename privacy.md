# Keybase-legal
App privacy
1. Information we collect
1.1 Information you provide directly

Account information:  
Name, username, email address, phone number, profile details.

Content you submit:  
Messages, files, images, notes, and any other content you upload or send through the App.

Support communications:  
Information you provide when you contact us for help or feedback.

1.2 Information collected automatically

Usage data:  
App features used, clicks, in‑app navigation, timestamps, crash logs, performance data.

Device information:  
Device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, and approximate location (based on IP address).

Log data:  
IP address, access times, pages/screens viewed, and other diagnostic data.

1.3 Optional permissions

Depending on how you use the App, we may request access to:

Camera & photos: To capture and upload images or videos.

Microphone: To record audio or voice messages.

Storage: To save and access files within the App.

Contacts: To help you find and connect with other users (only if you choose to enable this).

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

2. How we use your information
We use the information we collect to:

Provide and maintain the App  
Ensure core functionality, authentication, and account management.

Improve and personalize the experience  
Analyze usage to enhance features, performance, and usability.

Communicate with you  
Send service‑related notifications, updates, security alerts, and respond to support requests.

Ensure safety and security  
Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Use.

Comply with legal obligations  
Respond to lawful requests and enforce our rights.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Legal bases for processing (if applicable)
Where required by law (e.g., in the EU/EEA, UK), we process your personal data based on:

Performance of a contract – to provide the App and its features.

Legitimate interests – to improve the App, maintain security, and prevent misuse.

Consent – for optional features (e.g., contacts access, marketing communications).

Legal obligations – where we must comply with applicable laws.

4. How we share your information
We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers:  
With trusted third‑party vendors who help us operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics, crash reporting), under confidentiality and data‑protection obligations.

Legal and safety reasons:  
When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

Business transfers:  
In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data storage, security, and retention
Storage:  
Your data may be stored on servers located in various jurisdictions, depending on our hosting providers.

Security:  
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Retention:  
We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize your data.

6. Your rights and choices
Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

Access the personal information we hold about you.

Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

Delete your account and certain personal data.

Restrict or object to certain processing activities.

Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Portability – request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format (where applicable).

You can exercise many of these rights directly in the App (e.g., updating profile, deleting content) or by contacting us using the details below.

7. Children’s privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us.

8. International data transfers
Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, which may have different data‑protection laws. We take steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place when transferring data across borders, in accordance with applicable law.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top and, where required, notify you through the App or by other appropriate means. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated Policy.
